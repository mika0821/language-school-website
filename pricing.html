Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How do payments work?

All plans are billed every 4 weeks. You can pay via credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer. We send reminders 3 days before renewal.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer 10% discount for students and 15% for 3-month commitments. Contact us for family or group discounts.

What if I miss a class?

For private lessons, we offer makeup sessions with 24-hour notice. Group classes have recorded sessions available.
