Why Learn Spanish with YoLingo?

Spanish is the second most spoken language in the world with over 580 million speakers. At YoLingo, we bring the passion of Spanish culture into every lesson, from flamenco rhythms to historical discoveries, tapas culture to Latin American influences.

Native Spanish Teachers

All teachers are from Spain with DELE certification and university teaching degrees. They provide authentic accent and cultural context.

Interactive Cultural Events

Weekly conversation practice, cultural discussion groups, virtual tours of Spanish cities, cooking classes, and study sessions with peers.

Accelerated Learning

Proven methods that get you speaking confidently from your very first lesson. Our immersion approach accelerates language acquisition.

Course Levels

A1 - Principiante

Perfect for: Complete beginners taking their first steps in Spanish

You'll master: Essential greetings, basic self-introduction, simple questions, understanding everyday expressions, numbers, and basic vocabulary.

Goal: Communicate in basic social situations and understand simple Spanish.

Duration: 8 weeks (2 sessions/week)

A2 - Elemental

Perfect for: Those with some basic knowledge ready to expand

You'll master: Daily conversations, describing experiences, shopping interactions, understanding common texts, past and future tenses.

Goal: Handle routine tasks and conversations in Spanish-speaking environments.

Duration: 10 weeks (2 sessions/week)

B1 - Intermedio

Perfect for: Learners wanting to express complex ideas

You'll master: Expressing opinions, discussing current events, understanding Spanish media, workplace vocabulary, intermediate grammar.

Goal: Engage in meaningful conversations on diverse topics in Spanish.

Duration: 12 weeks (2 sessions/week)

B2 - Avanzado

Perfect for: Advanced speakers preparing for certification

You'll master: Complex debates, technical discussions, advanced grammar, understanding cultural nuances, professional writing.

Goal: Achieve fluency and prepare for DELE certification exams.

Duration: 16 weeks (2 sessions/week)
