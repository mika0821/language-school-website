Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I start lessons?

You can start within 48 hours of registration! We'll match you with a perfect teacher and schedule your first lesson. Most students have their first lesson within 24 hours.

Do you offer trial lessons?

Yes! Book a free 30-minute trial lesson to experience YoLingo's unique teaching style before committing. No credit card required for the trial.

Can I switch teachers?

Absolutely! If the teaching style doesn't match your learning preferences, we'll find you a better match within 24 hours at no extra cost.

What if I need to reschedule?

We offer flexible rescheduling with 24-hour notice. For private lessons, we guarantee make-up sessions. Group classes provide recorded sessions.
