Why Learn French with YoLingo?

French is spoken by over 300 million people worldwide and is the official language of 29 countries. At YoLingo, we don't just teach grammar - we immerse you in French culture, from Parisian art to historical landmarks, cinema to gastronomy.

Certified Native Teachers

All our French teachers are native speakers with DELF/DALF certification and teaching degrees from French universities. They bring authentic pronunciation and cultural insights.

Cultural Immersion Activities

Monthly French film nights, historical discussions, conversation clubs with cultural themes, virtual tours of French landmarks, and cooking classes featuring French cuisine.

Personalized Progress Tracking

Regular assessments and personalized feedback with detailed progress reports. Our adaptive learning system adjusts to your pace and focuses on your specific challenges.

Course Levels

A1 - Complete Beginner

Perfect for: Absolute beginners starting their French journey

You'll master: Essential greetings, basic conversations, introducing yourself, understanding simple instructions, numbers, dates, and daily vocabulary.

Goal: Hold basic conversations in everyday situations and understand simple French.

Duration: 8 weeks (2 sessions/week)

A2 - Elementary

Perfect for: Those with basic knowledge wanting to expand skills

You'll master: Daily life conversations, shopping, dining out, describing experiences, understanding common phrases, simple past and future tenses.

Goal: Navigate real-life situations confidently in French-speaking countries.

Duration: 10 weeks (2 sessions/week)

B1 - Intermediate

Perfect for: Learners ready for more complex conversations

You'll master: Expressing opinions, discussing current events, understanding French media, workplace communication, intermediate grammar structures.

Goal: Communicate effectively in professional and social settings.

Duration: 12 weeks (2 sessions/week)

B2 - Advanced

Perfect for: Advanced learners preparing for certification

You'll master: Complex debates, technical discussions, advanced writing, understanding nuances and idioms, professional correspondence.

Goal: Achieve fluency and prepare for DELF/DALF exams.

Duration: 16 weeks (2 sessions/week)
